About Ma'am Procees Torta Dalaguete

Our Story

Ma'am Procees Torta Dalaguete has been a beloved institution in Dalaguete, Cebu for over two decades. Founded by the passionate and talented Ma'am Procees, our bakery started as a small family operation with a simple mission: to create the most delicious tortas using traditional recipes passed down through generations.

What began as a humble stall in the local market has grown into a cherished community favorite, known for our authentic flavors and commitment to quality. Every torta we make is crafted with love, using only the freshest ingredients sourced from local farmers and suppliers.

Our Mission

To preserve and celebrate the rich culinary heritage of Cebu through our traditional torta recipes, while providing our customers with an unforgettable taste experience that brings joy to every bite.

Our Vision

To become the premier destination for authentic Cebuano tortas, expanding our reach while maintaining the warmth, quality, and tradition that our customers have come to love.

Why Choose Us?

  • Authentic Recipes: Our tortas follow time-honored recipes that have been perfected over years of dedication.
  • Fresh Ingredients: We use only the highest quality, locally sourced ingredients to ensure exceptional taste.
  • Family Tradition: Baking is in our blood, and we pour our passion into every product we create.
  • Community Focused: We're proud to be a part of the Dalaguete community and support local businesses.
